Biography

Shehaifa was born in Philippines to parents of Spanish descent and has been traveling extensively in Europe and the Middle East with her family whilst they were working on expatriate contracts, Shehaifa started her first dancing class at age of 10 in her school with Rhythmic Gymnastics. She carried on with this sport until the time of her collage graduation. Dance has been her passion since she was very young. Shehaifa has been involved in Spanish and Contemporary Dance since 1999. however, it was during her time in the Middle East that she discovered the “ Art of the Belly Dance and become completely fascinated by the exotic body rhythms, movement and music Shehaifa returned to the Middle East in 2021 and started taking classes and seminars under different master Belly Dance Instructor over a five years period and become a Certified Professional Belly Dance instructor and acclaimed overseas performer in Europe and Middle East in 2006. Shehaifa has for many years performed in an artistic. Elegant, Graceful and energetic style.

Which reflects her diverse background and personality. The passion and fascination continue to grow with every event. workshop and performance. She has choreograph many dance routines for both solo and groups and organized many dance events. she has performed in Middel East Theaters such as Madinat Theatre in Jumeirah Dubai, Art Center in Emirates Mall and Emirates’ Theatre Dubai, Also in Europe, South East Asia, Hongkong, Philippines Theaters and Featured On Philippine TV.

Travelling, Teaching and performing

Shehaifa has travelled and given Belly Dance workshops and performed in the following countries over the fast 8 years, Hawaii, England, Germany, Australia, Egypt, Qatar, Turkey, Bharain, Dubai, America, Belguim, France, Spain, Denmark, Sweden, Argentina, Netherland ,Thailand, Philippines Singapore and hongkong. Shehaifa will continue to travel to other countries to conduct workshops and perform in Bellydance Festivals during 2013 and beyond.
